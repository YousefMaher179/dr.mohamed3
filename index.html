Our Services

SASI BYPASS

Gastric sleeve is made together with gastric bypass by connecting stomach

exit with the intestine after passing a part of it. All this is done without separating

the stomach exit and so, There will be two ways for passage of food:*30% of food

passes in its normal pass way and so vitamins and minerals are normally absorbed.

*70% of food passes in gastric bypass pass way and so a lot of food and sugars are not

absorbed which results in weight loss and treatment of diabetes.
